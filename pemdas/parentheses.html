Parentheses

Parentheses is the first step of the order of operations. Complete all mathematical expressions within the parentheses before continuing to solve the expression! Inside the parentheses, however the order of operations must be followed. So, whatever is in the parentheses comes first!

Example!
6 X (5+3)
6 X 8
=48
