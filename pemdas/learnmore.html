Aunt Sally

Meet Aunt Sally! She is the Order of Operations Queen and will teach you each step of the process. The Order of Operations is a set of rules utilized to find the correct answer to mathematical expressions! In the mnemonic device Please Excuse My Dear Aunt Sally, each first word stands for a mathematical operation in order that each should be completed. For example, Please stands for Parentheses, Excuse stands for Exponents, My stands for Multiplication, Dear stands for Division, Aunt stands for Addition, and S stands for Subtraction. What part do you calculate first? Click home and go through each module to find out! Warning: Calculate them in the wrong order, and you can get a wrong answer.
