Multiplication

Multiplication is the third arthimetic process of the order of operations. If multiplication is inside parentheses, it is not completed until after all exponents are raised to their power. Multiplication is the middle step that precedes division, addition and subtraction. It is denoted by a smaller x, dot, or asterisk symbol between numbers.

Example!
2 + (5*3)
2 + 15
= 17
