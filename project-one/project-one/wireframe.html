Bangers / regular

Artist Vernon Adams created Bangers when he practiced typeface design from 2007 to 2014, before his death in(2016.) Adams heavily contributed many of Google and Adobe Font’s library with typefaces like Oswald and Pacifico(Bangers). Mid-century superhero comics served as Adam’s inspiration as the typeface’s cultural connotations cannot be ignored. I chose this typeface due to the cultural relevance and homage to pop culture.

Julia Stengle

Major: Film, Television, and Theater
